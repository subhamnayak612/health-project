Key Features for a Smarter System

This registry is more than just data storage; it's an active tool for public health management.

🛏 Real-Time Bed & Facility Registry

Granular, live status of every bed in every ward (vacant/occupied/reserved).

💊 Medicine

Click to search for medicines

📈 Unified Case & Vaccination Tracker

Each case linked to a unique patient ID (ABHA/Aadhaar) for a complete health profile.

🗺 Predictive Outbreak Dashboard

Real-time heatmaps and automated alerts for block/district level officials.

citizen_view Citizen View Portal

Public app/USSD/SMS to find nearest facility with beds, oxygen, vaccine or emergency blood requirement system

🔒 Patient Consent Layer

Aligned with ABDM, patients control who can access their personal health information.

🤖 AI-Powered Analytics

Forecasts demand for beds and medicines, enabling proactive resource management.
